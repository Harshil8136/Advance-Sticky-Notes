Help & Shortcuts

Core Actions

  • Click to Copy: Left-click any line to instantly copy its text.
  • Actions Menu: Right-click any line to open the menu for Edit, Delete, and Color options.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Command Palette: Press Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K on Mac) to open the powerful search tool.
  • Close Menus: Press Esc to close any open menu or modal.

Interface

  • Resize Panels: Drag the separator line between the two lists to resize them.
  • Panel Colors: Click the "DESCRIPTION" or "SUBJECT LINE" titles to change the background color of that panel.
